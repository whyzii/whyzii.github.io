Paced: Stutter Support
5.0 · Health & Fitness
iPhone · iPad · Apple Watch · Mac

A metronome-guided speech fluency app for people who stutter. The Gradual Fade mode slowly withdraws the metronome over time — the goal is to make it unnecessary, not a crutch. Includes an Apple Watch companion for wrist control and runs with zero data collection.

GraBeat: Play, Pinch, Repeat
5.0 · #79 Action
iPhone · iPad · Mac

A two-player rhythm game controlled by hand gestures — no buttons, no controllers. The camera tracks your hands in real time using Vision and you catch notes to the beat. Supports Versus and Co-op on one screen, with Game Center leaderboards and a built-in cyberpunk Photo Booth that saves shots locally.

About

I'm Abbas, an iOS developer based in Napoli. I work in Swift and SwiftUI, and I've shipped apps across health, accessibility, and games — three areas that don't have much overlap, which I think is the point.

Paced came from noticing there weren't many well-designed tools for people who stutter. GraBeat came from wanting to make something social and physical that required no setup at all. Both apps are free, private, and built without third-party dependencies.
