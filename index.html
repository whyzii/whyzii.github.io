About

I'm Abbas, an iOS developer based in Napoli. I work in Swift and SwiftUI, and I've built apps across health, games, and creative tools, three areas that don't have much overlap, which I think is the point.

Paced came from noticing there weren't many well-designed tools for people who stutter. GraBeat came from wanting to make something social and physical that required no setup at all. RapUp came from wanting a focused writing tool for songwriters that stays out of the way. All apps are free, private, and built without third-party dependencies.

I'm early in this and still exploring, open to new technologies and to the kind of work that has a reason behind it. Reach me at abbas.yousefzadeh98@gmail.com, find my code on GitHub, or connect on LinkedIn.
